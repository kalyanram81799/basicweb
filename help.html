Help & Support

Welcome to the Help Page

If you're facing any issues or need assistance, you're in the right place. Below are some common questions and their solutions. If your issue is not listed, feel free to contact us directly using the details provided below.

1. How to Book an Appointment?

To book an appointment, simply visit the Appointment Booking section of our website, select the hospital and the date that works best for you, and proceed with payment.

2. How to Contact Emergency Services?

If you're facing an emergency, call the emergency number provided in our website. We also provide direct hospital contact details based on your location to ensure immediate help.

3. How to Order Medicines?

For ordering medicines, visit our Medicine Delivery section, choose the medications you need, add your location, and proceed with the payment. You can opt for cash on delivery or online payment options.
