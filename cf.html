RIMS Hospital in Ongole, Andhra Pradesh, is a leading government healthcare facility offering comprehensive medical services. Established with a focus on providing quality healthcare, it caters to both inpatient and outpatient needs. The hospital is equipped with modern medical technology and a skilled team of doctors and specialists. Services include general medicine, surgery, pediatrics, obstetrics, cardiology, and emergency care.
RIMS Hospital also serves as a teaching institution, affiliated with a medical college, contributing to the education of future healthcare professionals. It is dedicated to offering affordable, accessible care to people from all walks of life, ensuring high standards in both clinical care and patient satisfaction. The hospital continues to be a trusted healthcare provider in the region.
